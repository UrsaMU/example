## Key Figures

Named individuals in the city's shadow politics. One-line each. Full pages
linked.

---

### Mortality

**[Gerald Fitch](npcs/gerald-fitch)** -- Church Network coordinator, St. Johns
pastor, 63, thirty years in, tired but not done.

---

### Night

**[Eleanor Vance](npcs/eleanor-vance)** -- The Night's senior figure. In
Portland since 1887. Remembers the original agreements. Inclined toward
patience; questioning whether patience still serves.

**[Marcus Cole](npcs/marcus-cole)** -- Night's day-facing representative. In
Portland since 1971. Runs intelligence in Old Town. Convinced the patience
strategy is failing and not yet sure what to do about it.

**[Ida Blackwell](npcs/ida-blackwell)** -- Ghost. Died 1934. Technically
neutral. Every faction defers to her on history and precedent. She appears when
she has something to say.

---

### Power

**[Thomas Aldrich](npcs/thomas-aldrich)** -- Rose Festival Foundation board
chair. Third-generation Portland. Has read the full Archive. Sleeps fine. Wants
the lid to stay on.

**[Priya Shetty](npcs/priya-shetty)** -- Tech-money, Foundation board member
since 2023. Sharp, asks good questions, being digested by Power without fully
understanding it yet.

---

### Missing

**[Rachel Voss](npcs/rachel-voss)** -- Forest Park eco-resort project manager.
Missing since September 2023. Listed as voluntary adult departure. Her family
does not believe this. They are right.
