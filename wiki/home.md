Welcome to City of Roses -- an Urban Shadows game set in Portland, Oregon.

Portland sells itself as weird, welcoming, green. That's the mask.

Underneath: a city with bones stained by old sins, a river that swallows
secrets, and a forest that belongs to something older than the city's name. The
roses grow wild here. They don't need tending. Nobody asks why anymore.

City of Roses is a game of dark intrigue, supernatural politics, and the deals
that keep everything from falling apart -- until they don't.
