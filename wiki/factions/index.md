## The Four Factions

City of Roses runs on four factions. They are not teams. They are not allies.
They are categories of interest that happen to require each other to survive.
Every significant NPC has a faction. Every deal has a faction angle.

- [Mortality](factions/mortality) - The human world that knows too much
- [Night](factions/night) - The old things in Old Town/Chinatown
- [Power](factions/power) - Old Portland money and its machines
- [Wild](factions/wild) - Forest Park and what lives in it

---

## Current Tensions

- Night vs Power: Pearl District expansion is eating the buffer zone between
  their territories. A sixty-year-old agreement is being lawyered by both sides.

- Wild vs Power: A proposed Forest Park eco-resort has sat in city planning for
  three years without moving. The developer's project manager disappeared eight
  months ago.

- Mortality (Foundation) vs Mortality (Press): The Portland Thorn has a source
  inside the Rose Festival Foundation. The Foundation knows. Neither side has
  moved yet.

- Mortality (Church) vs Night: Three Old Town vampires have gone missing in the
  last year. The Church Network won't confirm or deny. The Night is not asking
  politely.
